Research Plan: Locating 'The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy'

Steps:
1. Confirm Bibliographic Details:

   - Title: The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy
   - Ensure the correct edition (if specifically looking for a hardback, paperback, or special edition).

2. Online Availability Search:

   - Search aggregator sites like AbeBooks or ThriftBooks for used/rare editions.
   - Check digital platforms (Kindle, Audible, Apple Books) for digital/audio formats.

3. Local Availability:
   - Search local library catalogs to see if it is available for loan.
   - Use maps or directory tools to locate nearby independent bookstores or second-hand shops.

4. Execution & Tracking:
   - Document where the book was found.
   - If purchasing, compare prices across identified sources.
